# Pizza Sales Analysis Findings

## 1. Business Overview

- Total revenue: $817,860.05
- Total orders: 21,350
- Total pizzas sold: 49,574
- Average order value: $38.31

## 2. Menu Performance

### Highest-revenue pizza types

1. Thai Chicken Pizza
2. Barbecue Chicken Pizza
3. California Chicken Pizza
4. Classic Deluxe Pizza
5. Spicy Italian Pizza

### Lowest-selling pizza types

1. Brie Carre Pizza
2. Mediterranean Pizza
3. Calabrese Pizza
4. Spinach Supreme Pizza
5. Soppressata Pizza

### Category performance

- Highest-revenue category: Classic
- Lowest-revenue category: Veggie
- Main observation: Classic pizzas generated the most revenue overall, while Veggie pizzas generated the least. However, the highest-revenue individual pizza was the Thai Chicken Pizza, showing that the strongest individual product did not belong to the strongest overall category.

### Size performance

- Most frequently sold size: Large
- Highest-revenue size: Large
- Main observation: Large pizzas were both the most frequently purchased size and the highest-revenue size, making them the strongest-performing size in the menu.

## 3. Operational Performance

### Monthly performance

- Highest-revenue month: July 2015
- Lowest-revenue month: October 2015
- Main pattern: Monthly revenue varied throughout the year, with sales reaching their highest level in July and their lowest level in October.

### Weekday performance

- Busiest weekday: Friday
- Quietest weekday: Sunday
- Weekday with the highest average order value: Saturday
- Main observation: Friday recorded the highest number of orders, while Sunday had the lowest. Although Saturday was only the third-busiest day by order volume, it had the highest average order value, suggesting that Saturday customers placed larger or higher-value orders on average.

### Hourly performance

- Busiest hour: 12:00
- Quietest hour: 09:00
- Hour with the highest average pizzas per order: 09:00
- Main observation: Order volume was highest around lunchtime, especially at 12:00. Although 09:00 had the fewest orders, it had the highest average number of pizzas per order, suggesting that morning orders were less frequent but larger.

### Peak demand

- Three busiest hours: 12:00, 13:00, and 18:00
- Busiest weekday-and-hour combination: Thursday at 12:00
- Most popular category during the busiest hours: Classic

## 4. Initial Conclusions

1. Sales demand was concentrated around lunch and dinner, with 12:00, 13:00, and 18:00 producing the highest number of orders.

2. Classic pizzas and large pizzas were the strongest-performing menu groups, while the Thai Chicken Pizza generated the most revenue among individual pizza types.

3. Some low-volume periods still produced relatively large orders. In particular, 09:00 had the fewest orders but the highest average number of pizzas purchased per order.
